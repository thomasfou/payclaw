# SERVICE AGREEMENT

**Between:** Hiyaku ("Provider") and Crust ("Client")

**Effective Date:** ________________, 2026

---

## 1. Services

Provider agrees to supply the following services to Client:

- Hosting infrastructure for up to two (2) AI agents supporting Client operations
- Initial setup, configuration, and ongoing technical support for said agents
- Maintenance and uptime monitoring of hosted infrastructure

## 2. Term

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for three (3) months. The parties may extend this Agreement by mutual written consent.

## 3. Pricing

- **Infrastructure & Support:** Provided at no cost to Client.
- **LLM API Usage:** Client is responsible for all third-party AI/LLM costs (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic). These will be billed at cost with no markup. Client may provide API keys directly or reimburse Provider for usage incurred on Client's behalf.

## 4. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

- Provide or reimburse all LLM API and subscription costs in a timely manner
- Supply clear requirements and feedback regarding agent performance
- Be reasonably available for iteration and collaboration

## 5. Provider Responsibilities

Provider agrees to:

- Maintain reasonable uptime for hosted agent infrastructure
- Provide timely support for configuration changes and issues
- Communicate promptly regarding any service disruptions

## 6. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with fourteen (14) days' written notice to the other party. Upon termination, Provider will assist with a reasonable transition of any Client data or services.

## 7. Limitation of Liability

Services are provided "as is." Provider shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of the services. Provider's total liability shall not exceed the amounts paid by Client under this Agreement.

## 8. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to keep confidential any non-public information received from the other party and to use such information only for purposes of this Agreement.

- This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties regarding its subject matter.
- Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
